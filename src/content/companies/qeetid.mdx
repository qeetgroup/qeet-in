## Overview

Qeetid is the first venture inside Qeet Group. It started from a question every engineering team eventually asks: why is authentication this expensive, this complicated, and this divided between "simple developer tools" and "real enterprise platforms"? Qeetid is being built to close that gap — developer simplicity on the surface, enterprise depth underneath, at a price that doesn't force teams to choose between the two.

## What they build

A single authentication and authorization platform organized into six product lines: **Auth** (user identity and sessions), **ID** (identity profiles and lifecycle), **Access** (permissions and policies), **Guard** (anomaly detection and account protection), **Connect** (federation and SSO), and **Keys** (developer keys and machine identity). Teams adopt one line, then expand into adjacent ones as they grow.

## Who they serve

Developers building their first product. Startups adding their first compliance certifications. Enterprises consolidating identity infrastructure across business units. The point is the same product feels right at every stage — no migration when scale arrives.
